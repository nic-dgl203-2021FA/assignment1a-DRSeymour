Social Good Poster

This is a student project to learn skills in Adobe Illustrator.

This project was to research a graphic designer who inspired your and in their style, create a Poster to bring awareness to a social issue. The graphic designer I chose to imitate is Kate Moross because of her passion for candy wrapper packaging as a child. I too was inspired by the cartoonistic styling of many sweet treats!

My social awareness issue is to 'Save the Bees'. It is a world wide issue in that it reflects our food production everywhere as these pollinators travel far and wide. Modern pesticides and habitat destruction has resulted in an alarming reduction of these important little critters. We need to change the destructive habits and to accomplish this, media is necessary for awareness.

Certified Dental Assistant

Qualicum Beach Dental

October 2006 - March 2020

The position of 14.5 years included all regulated skills designated by The College of Dental Surgeons of BC (CDSBC). However, I also built their original website and I organized birthday acknowledgements and office outings.

Intra Oral Skills:

  • both analogue and digital radiographic exposure and processing, including pan, ceph & CBCT scans
  • coronal polishing, application of flu and pit/fissue sealants
  • matrix positioning and dental dam placement & removal
  • analoque and digital impressions

Extra Oral Skills:

  • reception duties, and digital charting
  • sterilization
  • lab work involving pouring/trimming models
  • fabrication of mouthguards
  • operating equipmemt for direct milling of Emax or Zircad crowns and/or printing acrylic surgical guides
